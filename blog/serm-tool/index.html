Implementation Project of a SERM Data Modeling Tool

2017, Sep 22

As a contribution to the PrOse 2017 workshop at PoEM 2017 I presented our paper on “Experiences from the Implementation of a Structured-Entity-Relationship Modeling Method in a Student Project”.

It was authored by a student team of Thilo Glässner, Florian Heumann and Luca Keßler as well as teachers Felix Härer, Andreas Steffan and Hans-Georg Fill.

Full Text / Presentation

As a result of the project, the implementation of the data modeling tool created by the student team is available for download at OMiLAB.

Citation:
Glässner, Thilo, Maximilian, Heumann, Florian, Keßler, Lucas, Härer, Felix, Steffan, Andreas, Fill, Hans-Georg (2017): Experiences from the Implementation of a Structured-Entity-Relationship Modeling Method in a Student Project, in: Bork, D., Karagiannis, D., Vanthienen, J.: Proceedings of the 1st International Workshop on Practicing Open Enterprise Modeling within OMiLAB (PrOse 2017) co-located with 10th IFIP WG 8.1 Working Conference on the Practice of Enterprise Modelling (PoEM 2027), CEUR Proceedings, http://ceur-ws.org/Vol-1999/.
